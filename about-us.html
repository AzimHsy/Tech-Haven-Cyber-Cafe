Discover the Journey of Azim Cafe

We are a group of enthusiastic students, embarking on an exciting project to design and plan a cyber café. This initiative combines creativity and technical expertise as we select hardware and software, design a network topology, configure IP addressing, and choose the best internet services. Our goal is to create a tech-driven space that caters to gaming, communication, and productivity needs. Through this website, we’ll showcase our plans, progress, and insights, providing a glimpse into how we’re transforming a simple shop lot into a vibrant cyber café. Thank you for joining us on this exciting journey of turning a simple shop lot into a tech-powered space for communication, gaming, and creativity!
